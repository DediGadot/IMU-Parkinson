## Route

The public Scientific Reports 2025 paper reports 10 people with PD undergoing a single-dose levodopa challenge while wearing ActiMyo magneto-inertial sensors on the most affected wrist and ankle. MDS-UPDRS Part III was collected before drug intake and then every 15 minutes for 90 minutes; the ActiMyo sensors sampled acceleration and angular velocity at 130.69 Hz.

The data are not directly downloadable. The paper states that academic, non-commercial access may be granted after review of a written research proposal and signature of a data access agreement.

## Decision

This is related work for wearable monitoring of pharmacological motor fluctuations, not an active WearGait-PD T1/T3 CCC route. The cohort is N=10, the target is a within-subject levodopa-challenge trajectory rather than cross-sectional severity, no T1 items 9-14 path is visible, and no row-level sensor/label schema is available.

No access runbook, preregistration, download, scaffold, request packet, or remote job is justified for the active objective.
